# Organization Billing Panel

The Billing tab panel of the Organization Profile — a tabbed shell hosting the Subscription, Statements, and Payments views. The Subscription tab lists an organization's subscriptions with their fees, seat usage, and next-payment overview, and owns the switch-plan and manage-subscription actions. It gates on `org:sys_billing:read` to view and `org:sys_billing:manage` to manage.
